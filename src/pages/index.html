Welcome to CCLE

CCLE (Common Collaboration and Learning Environment) is a campus-wide IT effort to converge upon a single platform for UCLA's web-based collaboration and learning needs. After conducting a detailed assessment of functional and technical requirements, UCLA decided to base CCLE upon Moodle, an open-source learning management system (LMS).
